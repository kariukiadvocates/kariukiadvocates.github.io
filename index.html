About the Firm

Yvonne W. Kariuki & Associates Advocates is a boutique law firm based in Nairobi, Kenya, committed to delivering tailored legal solutions in litigation, conveyancing, and commercial law. We believe in the power of personal attention, strategic thinking, and ethical advocacy.
