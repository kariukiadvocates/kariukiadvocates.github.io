About the Firm

Yvonne W. Kariuki & Associates Advocates is a boutique law firm based in Nairobi, Kenya, committed to delivering tailored legal solutions in the areas of litigation, conveyancing, and commercial law. We believe in the power of personal attention, strategic thinking, and ethical advocacy. Our goal is to help clients make informed decisions and achieve practical outcomes with professionalism and care.

We are deeply rooted in the values of integrity, diligence, and excellence — ensuring that every client feels supported, heard, and empowered.

Meet the Team

Yvonne W. Kariuki

Founder & Managing Partner

Yvonne is a passionate and dynamic advocate with a strong background in litigation, conveyancing, and commercial transactions. She was admitted to the bar on 8th March 2024 and leads the firm with a clear vision — to offer accessible, high-quality legal services with integrity and personal attention.

Her legal philosophy is rooted in client-centered service, with a strong emphasis on trust, transparency, and empowerment. Yvonne’s practice areas include civil litigation, family law, property law, and business transactions.

"For me, the practice of law is not just a profession — it’s a calling to serve, empower, and walk alongside those in search of justice."
